## Customize Collision Behavior Dynamically at Runtime

XAF includes a @DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.ProcessDataLockingInfoController. Use this controller to implement your own collision handling strategies. Handle the @DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.ProcessDataLockingInfoController.DataLockingProcessing event to manage information about modified records and collisions.
