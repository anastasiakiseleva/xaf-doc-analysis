We do not recommend using SQLite with XAF in **production** scenarios with large amounts of data. SQLite is a file-based database that, due to its design, is inferior in performance and other features to powerful distributed server-based RDBMSs such as SQL Server, Oracle, MySQL, or PostgreSQL (used by the majority of our customers). If you experience performance issues with SQLite and other file-based databases (even with our [Server Mode data sources](xref:8398)), we cannot offer any suitable solutions other than switching to another RDBMS.
