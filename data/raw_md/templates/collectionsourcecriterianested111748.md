In nested List Views, the **Criteria** is not directly applied to the associated collection. Instead, a separate collection is created, and the criteria are applied to it; the original associated collection is not modified. A nested List View in the **Client** [data access mode](xref:113683) with a disabled proxy collection (see [XafApplication.DefaultCollectionSourceMode](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.DefaultCollectionSourceMode)) is an exception. In this instance, the criteria are directly applied to the associated collection.
