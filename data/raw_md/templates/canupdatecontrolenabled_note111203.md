Set the **CanUpdateControlEnabled** protected property to **true** in the constructor of your custom Property Editor if it is required to update the [Control.Enabled](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/api/system.windows.forms.control.enabled#System_Windows_Forms_Control_Enabled) property value (e.g., when the underlying control is created or the [PropertyEditor.AllowEdit](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Editors.PropertyEditor.AllowEdit) property is changed).
