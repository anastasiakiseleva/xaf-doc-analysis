You can apply [RuleUniqueValue](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.RuleUniqueValueAttribute), [RuleObjectExists](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.RuleObjectExistsAttribute), [RuleIsReferenced](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.RuleIsReferencedAttribute), and [RuleCombinationOfPropertiesIsUnique](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.RuleCombinationOfPropertiesIsUniqueAttribute) rules to persistent business objects only because they search for objects within the application database. If the rule is applied to a base class that has descendants, the condition is tested against objects of all derived types.
