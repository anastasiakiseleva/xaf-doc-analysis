As the Show View Strategy may change during the application life cycle, we recommend that you subscribe to the `ShowViewStrategy` event from the [XafApplication.ShowViewStrategyChanged](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.ShowViewStrategyChanged) event handler in Windows Forms applications. For more information, refer to the following topic: [WinShowViewStrategyBase.WinWindowShowing](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinShowViewStrategyBase.WinWindowShowing).
