The @DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication class uses the **ISupportSplashTypes** interface to [show](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication.StartSplash(DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.SplashType)) or [stop](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication.StopSplash(DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.SplashType)) the default [Splash Screen](xref:10823), a [Wait Form](xref:10824) or a [Splash Image](xref:10825#create-and-show-splash-image-in-code). 

For more details on how to show splash forms, refer to the [Splash Forms](xref:112680) article.
