The **SplashType** enumeration's values specify a splash form for the [WinForms Splash Screen Manager](xref:10826) to show. The [WinApplication.StartSplash](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication.StartSplash(DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.SplashType)) method accepts the **SplashType** values as a parameter.
