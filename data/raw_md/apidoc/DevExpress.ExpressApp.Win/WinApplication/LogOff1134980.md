Used by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.LogoffController) Controller. Triggers the [XafApplication.LoggingOff](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LoggingOff) and [XafApplication.LoggedOff](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LoggedOff) events. You can handle the former to cancel the log off process, and the latter, to be notified when a user has logged off.
