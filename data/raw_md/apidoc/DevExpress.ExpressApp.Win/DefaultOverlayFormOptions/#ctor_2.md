This constructor creates an empty instance of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.DefaultOverlayFormOptions) class. 

If you call this constructor, the [WinForms Splash Screen Manager](xref:10826) uses default settings and the default painter to show an [Overlay Form](xref:120029). You cannot assign custom @DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.DefaultOverlayFormOptions.Options or a custom @DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.DefaultOverlayFormOptions.Painter to the Overlay form later.
