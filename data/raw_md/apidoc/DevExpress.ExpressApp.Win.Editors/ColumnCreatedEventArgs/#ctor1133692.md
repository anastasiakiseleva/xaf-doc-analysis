Since the **ColumnCreatedEventArgs** supplies arguments to the [WinColumnsListEditor.ColumnCreated](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.Editors.WinColumnsListEditor.ColumnCreated) event that is raised automatically by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.Editors.GridListEditor), you do not need to manually instantiate the **ColumnCreatedEventArgs**.
