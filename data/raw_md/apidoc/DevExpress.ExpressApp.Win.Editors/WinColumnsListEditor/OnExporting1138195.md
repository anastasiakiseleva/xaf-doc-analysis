The **OnExporting** method is exposed by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.IExportable) interface supported by the **[](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.Editors.WinColumnsListEditor)** class. This method is called by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.ExportController) before it starts to export the current exportable List Editor's [IExportable.Printable](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.IExportable.Printable) control.
