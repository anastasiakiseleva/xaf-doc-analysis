The **PrintableChanged** event is exposed by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.IExportable) interface supported by the **[](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.Editors.WinColumnsListEditor)** class. This event is raised when the GridListEditor's [WinColumnsListEditor.Grid](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.Editors.WinColumnsListEditor.Grid) control is created and disposed of.
