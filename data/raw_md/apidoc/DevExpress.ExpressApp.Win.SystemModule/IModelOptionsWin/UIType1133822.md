We do not recommend setting this property to **StandardMDI** when the [Ribbon](xref:404212) interface is used. This configuration has not been thoroughly tested, as we have found it much less usable than others, and can potentially lead to errors. In conjunction with the Ribbon, the **TabbedMDI** mode provides better usability.
