You can change this property value in the [Model Editor](xref:112582).

![WinForms-IModelApplicationOptionsSkin-Skin](~/images/WinForms-IModelApplicationOptionsSkin-Skin.png)

If you choose an SVG skin, the [Palette](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.SystemModule.IModelApplicationOptionsPalette.Palette) property becomes available.

In [LightStyle](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication.UseLightStyle), XAF displays new WinForms [runtime skin selectors](xref:2399) for end-users.
