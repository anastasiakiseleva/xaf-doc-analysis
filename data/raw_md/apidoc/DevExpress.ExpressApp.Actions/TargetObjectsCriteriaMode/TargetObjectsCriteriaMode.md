Members of the **TargetObjectsCriteriaMode** enumeration specify the selection type that must by applied to a View to enable a specific Action. To get the enumeration value which is considered by the Action, use the [ActionBase.TargetObjectsCriteriaMode](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ActionBase.TargetObjectsCriteriaMode) property.
