Currently, members of the `IContainer` interface are implemented in the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Controller) class only. The `container`  parameter is used to initialize the [ActionBase.Controller](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ActionBase.Controller) property of the created Pop-up Window Show Action.

Usually, you do not need to create [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.PopupWindowShowAction) objects manually. For details, refer to the [Actions](xref:112622) topic.
