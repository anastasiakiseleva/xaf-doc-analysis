Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SingleChoiceActionExecuteEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [SingleChoiceAction.Execute](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SingleChoiceAction.Execute) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your application.
