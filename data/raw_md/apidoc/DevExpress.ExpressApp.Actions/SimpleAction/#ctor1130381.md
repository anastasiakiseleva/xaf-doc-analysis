Basicaly, all Actions are contained and managed by [Controllers](xref:112621) specified by their [ActionBase.Controller](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ActionBase.Controller) property. This constructor creates a Simple Action and sets **null** for its [ActionBase.Controller](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ActionBase.Controller) property.

Usually, you do not need to create [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SimpleAction) objects manually. Instead, use the parent Controller's **Designer** to add Actions. For details, refer to the [Actions](xref:112622) topic.
