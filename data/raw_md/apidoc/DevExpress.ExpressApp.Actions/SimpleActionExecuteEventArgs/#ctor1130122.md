    description: An instance of the class that implements the **ISelectionContext** interface. Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SimpleActionExecuteEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [SimpleAction.Execute](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SimpleAction.Execute) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
