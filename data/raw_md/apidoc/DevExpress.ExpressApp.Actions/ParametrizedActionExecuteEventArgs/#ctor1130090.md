Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ParametrizedActionExecuteEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [ParametrizedAction.Execute](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ParametrizedAction.Execute) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your application.
