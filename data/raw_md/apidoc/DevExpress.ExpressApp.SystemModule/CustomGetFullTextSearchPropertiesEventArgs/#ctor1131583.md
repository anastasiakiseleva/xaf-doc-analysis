The **CustomGetFullTextSearchPropertiesEventArgs** objects are used to supply arguments to the [FilterController.CustomGetFullTextSearchProperties](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.FilterController.CustomGetFullTextSearchProperties) event. This event is triggered automatically by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.FilterController). So, you do not need to use this constructor.
