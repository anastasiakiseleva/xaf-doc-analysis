If you do not want to implement this interface from scratch, inherit the XPO [](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.BaseImpl.PermissionPolicy.PermissionPolicyUser) or EF Core [](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.BaseImpl.EF.PermissionPolicy.PermissionPolicyUser) class.
