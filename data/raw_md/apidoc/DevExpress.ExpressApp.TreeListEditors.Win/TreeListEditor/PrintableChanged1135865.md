The **PrintableChanged** event is exposed by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.IExportable) interface supported by the **[](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.TreeListEditors.Win.TreeListEditor)** class. This event is raised when the TreeListEditor's [TreeListEditor.TreeList](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.TreeListEditors.Win.TreeListEditor.TreeList) control is created and disposed of.
