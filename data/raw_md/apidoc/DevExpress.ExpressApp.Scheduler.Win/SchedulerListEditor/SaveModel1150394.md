Generally, you do not have to call this method.

Override this method to save the current settings of a List Editor's control to the [Application Model](xref:112580)'s node specified by the List Editor's [ListEditor.Model](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Editors.ListEditor.Model) property.
