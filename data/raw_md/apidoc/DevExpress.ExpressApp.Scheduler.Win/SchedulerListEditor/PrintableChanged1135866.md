The **PrintableChanged** event is exposed by the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.SystemModule.IExportable) interface supported by the **[](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Scheduler.Win.SchedulerListEditor)** class. This event is raised when the SchedulerListEditor's [SchedulerListEditor.SchedulerControl](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Scheduler.Win.SchedulerListEditor.SchedulerControl) control is created and disposed of.
