When implementing the [](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.IRuleSource) interface, use this property to return a unique string identifying your Validation Rule Source.

To see and example of implementing the **IRuleSource** interface, refer to the "Custom Rule Source" section of the [Declare Validation Rules](xref:113251) topic.
