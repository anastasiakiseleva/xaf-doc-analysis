---
uid: DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.IRuleSet.DropCachedRules
name: DropCachedRules()
type: Method
summary: Drops the Rule cache for all persistent Rule Sources.
syntax:
  content: void DropCachedRules()
seealso: []
---

To drop the Rule cache for a specific persistent Rule Source, find a particular `PersistentObjectRuleSource` or `PersistentContainerRuleSource` instance in the [IRuleSet.RegisteredSources](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.IRuleSet.RegisteredSources) collection and call the [IRuleSet.DropCachedRules()](xref:DevExpress.Persistent.Validation.IRuleSet.DropCachedRules) method.
