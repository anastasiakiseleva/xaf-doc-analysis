The **LastLogonParametersReadEventArgs** class declares properties specific to the [XafApplication.LastLogonParametersRead](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LastLogonParametersRead) event. This event is designed to customize the logon parameters loaded from a storage to the object specified by the handler's [LastLogonParametersReadEventArgs.LogonObject](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.LastLogonParametersReadEventArgs.LogonObject) parameter.
