Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.DetailViewCreatingEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [XafApplication.DetailViewCreating](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.DetailViewCreating) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
