Generally, you do not need to handle this event. To customize the current [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Frame)'s [Frame.Template](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Frame.Template), handle the [Frame.TemplateChanged](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Frame.TemplateChanged) event.
