Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.LogonFailedEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [XafApplication.LogonFailed](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LogonFailed) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
