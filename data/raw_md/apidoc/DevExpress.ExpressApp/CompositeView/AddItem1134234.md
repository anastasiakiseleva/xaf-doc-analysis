Adding a View Item to the [CompositeView.Items](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.CompositeView.Items) collection raises the [CompositeView.ItemsChanged](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.CompositeView.ItemsChanged) event.

Generally, you do not need to use this method. Instead, add View Items via the [Application Model](xref:112580). For this purpose, use the Application | Views | DetailView | Items node.
