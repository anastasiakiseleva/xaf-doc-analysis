Generally, you do not need to instantiate a [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ShowViewSource) object. If you need to display a [View](xref:112611) in a UI, use the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.PopupWindowShowAction) or the [ActionBaseEventArgs.ShowViewParameters](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.ActionBaseEventArgs.ShowViewParameters) parameter in an [Action](xref:112622)'s **Execute** event handler.
