The Visual Studio .NET design-time environment calls this method to finish initializing the application. The [XafApplication.BeginInit](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.BeginInit) method starts the initialization, and the **EndInit** method finishes it. Use the **BeginInit** and **EndInit** methods to prevent the application from being used until it has been completely initialized.
