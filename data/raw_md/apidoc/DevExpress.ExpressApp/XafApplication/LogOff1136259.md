This method is a base platform-independent method for the [WinApplication.LogOff](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Win.WinApplication.LogOff) and [BlazorApplication.LogOff](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Blazor.BlazorApplication.LogOff) methods. Use this method if you need to perform the LogOff operation from a platform-independent module.
