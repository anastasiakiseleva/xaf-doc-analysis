Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.LogonEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [XafApplication.LoggingOn](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LoggingOn) and [XafApplication.LoggedOn](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.LoggedOn) events, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
