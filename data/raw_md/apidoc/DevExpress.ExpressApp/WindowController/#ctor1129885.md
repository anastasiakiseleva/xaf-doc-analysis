Do not instantiate Controllers via this constructor. To create a Controller in code, use the [XafApplication.CreateController\<ControllerType>](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.CreateController``1) method.

To implement a custom View Controller, use the ready-to-use Controller templates supplied with XAF (see [Controllers](xref:112621)).
