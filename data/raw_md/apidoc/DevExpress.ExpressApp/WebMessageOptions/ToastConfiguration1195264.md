When the  **ToastConfiguration** value is specified, other **MessageOptions** properties are ignored. To learn more on how to generate correct settings for the **dxToast** widget, refer to the [Configuration](xref:js-DevExtreme.ApiReference.UI_Components.dxToast.Configuration) topic.
