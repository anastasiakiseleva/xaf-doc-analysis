Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ViewCreatingEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [Frame.ViewChanging](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Frame.ViewChanging) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
