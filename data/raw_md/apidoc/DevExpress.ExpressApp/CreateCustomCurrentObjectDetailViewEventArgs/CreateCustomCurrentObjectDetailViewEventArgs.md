The **CreateCustomCurrentObjectDetailView** event occurs when the current List View is updated, and when its current object is changed. Handle this event, to specify a custom Detail View to be displayed in the [MasterDetailMode.ListViewAndDetailView](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.MasterDetailMode.ListViewAndDetailView) mode.
