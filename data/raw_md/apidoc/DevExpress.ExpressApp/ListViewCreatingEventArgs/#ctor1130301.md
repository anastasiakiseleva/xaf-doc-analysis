Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ListViewCreatingEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [XafApplication.ListViewCreating](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.ListViewCreating) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
