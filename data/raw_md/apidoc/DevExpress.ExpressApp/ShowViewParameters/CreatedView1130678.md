To create a View, use the [CreateListView](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.CreateListView*), [CreateDetailView](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.CreateDetailView*) or [ProcessShortcut](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.ProcessShortcut(DevExpress.ExpressApp.ViewShortcut)) method.
