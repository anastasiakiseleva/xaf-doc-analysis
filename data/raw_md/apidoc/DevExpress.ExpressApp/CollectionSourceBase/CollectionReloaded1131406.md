name: CollectionReloaded
type: Event
summary: Occurs after the Collection Source's [CollectionSourceBase.Collection](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.CollectionSourceBase.Collection) has been reloaded.

  content: public event EventHandler CollectionReloaded

This event is raised by the [CollectionSourceBase.Reload](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.CollectionSourceBase.Reload) method. Handle the **CollectionReloaded** event to perform the required actions after the Collection Source's collection has been repopulated with data from the database.
