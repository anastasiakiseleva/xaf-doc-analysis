Since instances of the [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ViewShowingEventArgs) class are automatically created and passed to handlers of the [XafApplication.ViewShowing](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.XafApplication.ViewShowing) event, you do not need to call this constructor from your applications.
