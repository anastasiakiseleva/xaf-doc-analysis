This event occurs when the data records list is refreshed, sorted or filtered and when the columns set is changed. Introduced to provide [](xref:System.ComponentModel.IBindingList) support. For details on this event, refer to the [IBindingList.ListChanged Event](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dotnet/api/system.componentmodel.ibindinglist.listchanged) topic.
