A custom [](xref:DevExpress.XtraEditors.XtraForm) should contain the [](xref:DevExpress.DashboardWin.DashboardDesigner) and a ribbon or a toolbar menu. Use the [DashboardDesigner.CreateRibbon](xref:DevExpress.DashboardWin.DashboardDesigner.CreateRibbon) method or a [DashboardDesigner.CreateBars](xref:DevExpress.DashboardWin.DashboardDesigner.CreateBars) method to create the menu in code. Alternatively, use the approach described in the [Create a WinForms Designer](xref:12137) topic.
