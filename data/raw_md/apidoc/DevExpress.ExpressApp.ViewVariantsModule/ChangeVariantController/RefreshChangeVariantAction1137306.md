The **RefreshChangeVariantAction** method uses the [ChangeVariantController.CurrentFrameViewVariantsManager](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ViewVariantsModule.ChangeVariantController.CurrentFrameViewVariantsManager) property value to recreate [ChangeVariantController.ChangeVariantAction](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.ViewVariantsModule.ChangeVariantController.ChangeVariantAction) Action items and initialize this Action's [SingleChoiceAction.SelectedItem](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Actions.SingleChoiceAction.SelectedItem) property.
