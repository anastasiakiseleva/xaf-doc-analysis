List Editors are used by [List Views](xref:112611) to display object collections in a UI. Charting List Editors are designed to display and manage serial data in an efficient manner. `ChartListEditorBase` is the abstract base class from which the built-in WinForms-specific [](xref:DevExpress.ExpressApp.Chart.Win.ChartListEditor) derives.
