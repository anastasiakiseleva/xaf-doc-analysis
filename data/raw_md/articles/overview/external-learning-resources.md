# External Learning Resources

To learn more about XAF, you can refer to the external resources listed in this topic, in addition to browsing XAF documentation.

* [XAF](https://www.devexpress.com/Products/NET/Application_Framework/)

    XAF home page. Here, you can find a lot of information on the framework.

* [XAF Demos](https://www.devexpress.com/Support/Demos/#xaf)

    Online and offline demo applications we ship for XAF.


* [DevExpress Support Center](https://supportcenter.devexpress.com/)

    Use our Support Center to ask a question or to check if our support team has answered a similar question in the past.

* [XAF Development Blog](https://community.devexpress.com/blogs/xaf/)

    Share feedback to help shape our future development or read the latest documentation updates and development news.
    
* [XAF Community, Social Networks, Consulting and Training Classes](https://www.devexpress.com/products/net/application_framework/#community)

    Get in touch with XAF team and developers who use XAF on a day-to-day basis or get XAF-trained by third-parties.
